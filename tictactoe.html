Privacy Policy for TicTacToe Game

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Company: CODEBREW

📱 About the App

“TicTacToe” is an offline mobile game that allows users to play:

  • Human vs Human
  • Human vs AI Bot

This app is designed for all ages and does not require an internet connection to function.

🔐 Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No account registration or login is required
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone) are collected
  • No location, contacts, media, or any files are accessed
  • No analytics tools are integrated

🔑 Permissions

The app does not request or use any device permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Storage

🧠 AI Functionality

The AI gameplay feature runs entirely on-device and does not connect to any servers or cloud-based systems.

🌐 Internet Usage

The app functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection for any core functionality. No network requests are made.

👶 Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for children. Since no data is collected, it complies with COPPA and similar child data protection regulations.

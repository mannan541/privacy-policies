Delete Your GoGrocer Account

At GoGrocer, your privacy and data control are important to us. If you wish to delete your account and associated personal data, you may request it by emailing us.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to support@codebrew.ltd from the email address associated with your GoGrocer account. Include your full name and any details that can help us verify your account.

Once verified, your account and all associated personal data will be deleted from our Firebase servers within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • User authentication data (email, name)
  • Order history
  • Delivery and profile information

What Data is Retained?

We do not retain any personal data once the deletion request is processed.
